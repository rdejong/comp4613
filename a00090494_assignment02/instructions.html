address book help page


-

  • How to add a customer

    It's easy! Navigate to the start page from the Address Book Portal Page

    • Fill out the form
    • Click on add customer

  • How to modify a customer

    There are two ways to do this

    • Click on modify customer button beside the customer you want to modify in the customer table from the start page
    • Search for the customer by Id and the pre populated form will appear, make your changes and submit the form

  • How to remove a customer

    It's easy! Click on the delete customer button on the start page customer table and your done.

  • How to lookup a customer

    All customers are initially visible in the customer table in the start page - to view a single customer you can search by customer Id in from the index page
